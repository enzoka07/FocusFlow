Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

In short: FocusFlow stores all your data exclusively on your device. No personal data is transmitted to external servers or shared with third parties.

1. Controller

The controller within the meaning of the GDPR is:
Enzo Kaiser
Germany
Email: hello.aerohq@gmail.com

2. What data is processed?

Data stored locally on your device

FocusFlow stores the following data exclusively on your device (UserDefaults / local storage):

  • Tasks, descriptions, priorities, and due dates
  • Habits and their completion history
  • Focus sessions and time tracking data
  • Your user profile (name, points, level, daily goals)
  • App settings and notification preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not viewed, collected, or processed by us.

Data for in-app purchases (Apple)

Purchases and subscriptions are processed exclusively via Apple's StoreKit. We do not receive any payment data. Apple processes transaction data in accordance with the Apple Privacy Policy.

Crash reports

In the event of an app crash, Apple may send us anonymized crash reports. These do not contain any personally identifiable information.

3. Purpose of data processing

Locally stored data is used solely to operate the app – to display, manage, and analyze your tasks, habits, and focus sessions.

4. Notifications

When you enable notifications, local push notifications are scheduled on your device. This data is not transmitted. You can disable notifications at any time under iOS Settings → FocusFlow.

5. Your rights (GDPR)

Since we do not store personal data on our servers, all data is directly under your control. You have the following options:

  • Delete all app data: Profile → Delete All Data
  • Complete data deletion: Uninstall the app
  • Request information about stored data: Contact us by email

Under the GDPR, you have the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, and objection. For requests, please contact: hello.aerohq@gmail.com

6. Data security

Your data is stored locally on your device and protected by iOS security mechanisms (device encryption, app sandbox). We recommend securing your device with a passcode.

7. Children

FocusFlow is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy as needed. The current version is always available on this page. For significant changes, we will notify you via an app update.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, reach us at:
hello.aerohq@gmail.com

10. Right to lodge a complaint

You have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority. If you are located in Germany, the competent authority can be found at: www.bfdi.bund.de
