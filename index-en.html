FAQ

What is FocusFlow Premium?

FocusFlow Premium unlocks all advanced features – unlimited tasks, habits, detailed statistics, and more. Available as a monthly or yearly subscription.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime through iOS Settings: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → FocusFlow.

How do I restore my purchases?

Open FocusFlow → Profile → Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Is my data stored in the cloud?

Your data is stored locally on your device only. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Can I use FocusFlow on multiple devices?

FocusFlow can be used on all your iOS devices linked to the same Apple ID.

I have a problem – what should I do?
